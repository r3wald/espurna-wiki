## Introduction

* Input voltage: AC100-240V 50/60Hz
* Working Current: 10A
* Has power meter: NO
* Power consuming: ≤0.3W
* Dimensions L2.96 x W1.57 x H0.98"
* Has a USB port which alledgedly outputs 2.1A (it's also used for supplying power when flashing)
